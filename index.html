Charlie Wu

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the mathematics department at the University of Toronto, supervised by Daniel Litt. I am interested in algebraic geometry, especially in the study of local systems from the perspective of non-abelian Hodge theory. 

Tangentially, I have also spent some time thinking about Gromov-Witten theory and mapping class group actions on character varieties.
